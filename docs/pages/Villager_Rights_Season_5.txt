_Season 5_ is an ongoing season on the Villager Rights SMP that started on Saturday at February 6, 2021 at 12pm EST. It has spanned 2 major updates so far, those being the Nether Update and the Caves and Cliffs Update Part 1. Season 5 is set to end on the release of Caves and Cliffs Part 2.
Season 5 has been the most successful season so far, with players active throughout the season's lifetime, and a steady stream of new players.
